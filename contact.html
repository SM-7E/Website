Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We are currently focused on serving homeowners in Nashik, Pune, and surrounding regions in Maharashtra. We are planning to expand to other cities soon!

How do I start the renovation process with EcoRevive?

The first step is to fill out the contact form above or give us a call. We'll schedule a free, no-obligation consultation to discuss your vision, assess your home, and provide a preliminary quote.

Is a green renovation more expensive than a traditional one?

While some sustainable materials can have a higher upfront cost, we focus on solutions that provide significant long-term savings on your electricity and water bills. We work with a range of budgets and can help you take advantage of government subsidies to make your project more affordable.
